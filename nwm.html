Nantucket Whaling Museum Branding

The goal of the new identity was to bring the museum’s look up to date, but with some antique elements. The result nods to the classic style of the Nantucket Historical Society’s logo and uses the idea of a ship porthole with an image of a whale at its center. Solid, nautical inspired colors and an old style typeface play up the classic and modern existing together to compliment one another in this identity.

I wanted my graphics manual to present all the technical information about how to use the identity in the most clear way possible
